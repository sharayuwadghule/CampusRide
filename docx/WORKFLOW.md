# CampusRide User Workflow Recap

After a successful login, the user enters a comprehensive campus carpooling ecosystem. The application is designed to be dual-purpose, allowing students to switch between being a **Rider** (the one offering the ride) and a **Passenger** (the one seeking a ride).

## 1. Landing: The Home Dashboard
Upon login, users land on the **Home Screen**, which serves as the central hub:
- **Quick Switch**: Immediate buttons to either "Find a Ride" or "Offer a Ride".
- **Activity Summary**: A glance at upcoming or active rides.
- **Tonal Navigation**: Smooth bottom tabs for Home, My Rides, Notifications, and Profile.

---

## 2. The Rider Workflow (Offering a Ride)
For students with vehicles looking to share their commute:
1. **Initiation**: Tap "Offer a Ride".
2. **Details**: Specify the pickup point, drop-off, timing, and available seats.
3. **Fare Preview**: The app calculates a suggested fare based on distance to ensure costs are shared fairly.
4. **Active Offering**: Once posted, the ride becomes visible to potential passengers.
5. **Manage Requests**: The rider receives notifications as students request seats. They can view profiles before accepting.
6. **Execution**: During the ride, the rider uses the **Active Ride** view to manage the journey.
7. **Completion**: After reaching the destination, the rider confirms completion, views the summary, and rates the passenger.

---

## 3. The Passenger Workflow (Finding a Ride)
For students looking for a safe and affordable way to travel:
1. **Search**: Tap "Find a Ride" and enter destination details.
2. **Discovery**: View a list of available student-drivers heading the same way.
3. **Selection**: Inspect rider ratings, vehicle type, and estimated arrival time.
4. **Booking**: Send a ride request.
5. **Confirmation**: Once accepted, the passenger receives rider details (vehicle number, name) and real-time status.
6. **Journey**: Secure travel with verified campus peers.
7. **Payment & Rating**: Seamlessly handle the shared cost and provide feedback on the rider.

---

## 4. Safety & Profile Management
The workflow is underpinned by safety and personalization:
- **Verified Network**: All users must upload a student ID for verification during onboarding.
- **SOS Features**: High-priority SOS buttons are available for immediate emergency activation.
- **My Rides**: A historical log of all past commutes for easy tracking.
- **Profile Customization**: Users can update their vehicle details, college information, and contact preferences.

---

## 5. Logging Out
- Users can securely end their session from the **Profile Tab**.
- The logout process clears the session and returns the user to the starting **Welcome Screen**, ensuring account security on shared or public devices.
